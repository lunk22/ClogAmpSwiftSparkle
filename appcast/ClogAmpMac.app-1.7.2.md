# 1.7.2

- **Position Timeline**

  The seek slider in the player has been replaced by a visual timeline bar that shows all dance positions at their exact time positions. A blue fill indicates playback progress, a white playhead line marks the current position, and each named position appears as a tick mark with its label below the bar. You can click or drag anywhere on the bar to seek.

  The timeline is enabled by default. To switch back to the plain slider, go to Settings > General and uncheck "Show Timeline".

- **Customizable Keyboard Shortcuts**

  All keyboard shortcuts can now be configured freely in Settings > Shortcuts. Shortcuts are organised into sections (File, Edit, Tools, Player, and Others) and each action shows its current binding. Click a binding field and press the desired key combination to reassign it; click it again while it is active to clear the shortcut entirely.

  **Notable shortcuts in the "Others" section:**
  - **Jump to Position 1–10:** Press the corresponding digit key (1–0 on the number row) to jump directly to that position.
  - **Jump to Position 11–20:** Hold Shift while pressing the digit key.
  - **Play Position / Load Song:** Press Enter to play the selected position or load the selected song.

  Use the "Reset to Defaults" button at the bottom of the Shortcuts settings page to restore all shortcuts to their factory values.
